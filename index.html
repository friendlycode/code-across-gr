Exploring open data in our city

We’ll imagine the possibilities of having open data and transparency in Grand Rapids. Our speaker will introduce how open data has impacted their city. Then, we’ll get to work building upon and implementing our own ideas.

Calling dedicated Rapidians

If you’re a citizen, community leader, government official, coder, designer, planner or friend of Grand Rapids, we need your support! We work better when we work together— discuss, share, collaborate.

Code Across America 2014

A national weekend of civic innovation. Code Across Grand Rapids is just one of a number of events taking place around the world, gathering citizens passionate about technology and their communities. Check out all the Code Across and Open Data Day events going on around the world.
